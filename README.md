## 💻 Cybersecurity & Tech Risk Projects

*Projects detailing practical application of industry-standard security frameworks to protect enterprise infrastructure.*

### 🎯 [Project 1: NIST SP 800-53 Risk Assessment & Mitigation](./nist-risk-assessment/)
* **Focus:** Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) & Framework Alignment
* **Overview:** A simulated case study evaluating an enterprise migrating a legacy high-stakes operational workflow over to a cloud-based digital assessment architecture serving **4 million end-users annually**. 
* **Key Components:** Core vulnerability identification, risk rating matrices, and control design mapped directly to **NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5** (AC, SI, AT, and CP control families).

### 📘 [Project 2: Enterprise Security Incident Response Playbook](./incident-response/)
* **Focus:** Technical Risk Governance & Business Continuity
* **Overview:** A formal incident handling blueprint structured around **NIST SP 800-61 Rev. 2** guidelines, designed to mitigate a large-scale Ransomware outbreak and potential data exfiltration scenario.
* **Key Components:** Incident lifecycle governance (Preparation through Post-Incident Lessons Learned), RACI alignment, containment strategies, and audit-ready forensic documentation management.
